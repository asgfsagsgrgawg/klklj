Why Ghana

Accra — Africa's Most Exciting City

Accra is not the city you imagine from Abidjan, Dakar or Cotonou. It is more structured, more accessible, and full of opportunities that most Francophones have not yet discovered.

01

Safest in West Africa

Ghana is one of the most politically stable and secure countries on the continent. Residential areas like East Legon, Labone and Cantonments are exceptionally peaceful.

02

Strong Investment Returns

Gross rental yields of 8 to 12% in well-chosen zones. Expat rental demand is strong and constant. The market welcomes foreign investors with a transparent legal framework.

03

World-Class Schools

International schools for every curriculum — Lycée Français d'Accra, Lincoln Community School (American/IB), British International School and more. Your children will not be lost here.

04

Vibrant Food & Culture

A true gastronomic city. Ghanaian cuisine, Lebanese, Indian, European — and a weekend brunch culture that rivals any major city. If you love good food, you will not be disappointed.

Practical Notes

Eat local first

A Ghanaian can live comfortably on roughly 150,000 FCFA per month. Imported products are expensive — a baguette costs 1,000 FCFA versus 200 FCFA at home.

Health insurance

Private facilities offer good care. An international health insurance policy is strongly recommended before arriving in Ghana.

Connectivity

Mobile coverage is excellent. Buy a local SIM at the airport on arrival. Fibre optic internet is available in most residential neighbourhoods.

International schools

Lycée Français d'Accra (French curriculum), Lincoln Community School (American / IB), British International School, SOS Hermann Gmeiner. Your children will adapt very well.

Invest in Ghana

Real Estate Investment, Simplified

01

Leasehold & Freehold

In Ghana, land traditionally belongs to chiefs or the State. Most transactions are leasehold — long-term leases of 50 to 99 years. It is legal, secure and common practice.

02

Due Diligence

Always verify the land title before any purchase. Land disputes exist. Professional guidance avoids the classic pitfalls — we handle this entirely on your behalf.

03

Strong Rental Yields

Gross yields of 8 to 12% in well-chosen areas. Expat rental demand is strong and constant in Accra. We identify the best zones and connect you with certified developers.
